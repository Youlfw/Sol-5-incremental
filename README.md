# Sol-5 Incremental Game

Welcome to **Sol-5 Incremental**, an engaging incremental/clicker game where your goal is to reach the distant sun called Sol-5!

## 🎮 Game Overview

Sol-5 Incremental is a browser-based incremental game featuring exponential growth mechanics, multiple prestige layers, and various currencies. Start small by clicking for energy, buy generators to automate production, purchase upgrades to increase efficiency, and prestige for massive multipliers!

## 💰 Currencies

### Primary Currencies
- **Energy (⚡)** - Main currency earned from generators and clicks
- **Matter (🪨)** - Secondary resource generated from energy conversion
- **Light-Years (💫)** - Distance metric tracking progress toward Sol-5

### Prestige Currencies
- **Essence (✨)** - First prestige currency, gained by resetting with Energy Prestige
- **Celestial Shards (💎)** - Second prestige currency, gained by resetting with Cosmic Prestige

## ⚙️ Generators

Generate passive income automatically! Each generator scales with cost exponentially.

| Generator | Base Production | Base Cost |
|-----------|-----------------|-----------|
| Solar Collector | 0.1/sec | 10 |
| Matter Converter | 0.5/sec | 100 |
| Light Harvester | 0.2/sec | 500 |
| Quantum Reactor | 1/sec | 5,000 |
| Stellar Engine | 5/sec | 50,000 |

**Cost Formula:** Base Cost × 1.15^(owned)

## 🚀 Upgrades

11 unique upgrades to enhance your production:
- 3 Efficiency upgrades
- 2 Matter boosts
- 1 Light-Year boost
- 2 Prestige bonuses
- 2 Special multipliers
- 1 Advanced unlock feature

## 🌟 Prestige Layers

### Layer 1: Energy Prestige
Reset your progress to gain **Essence** multipliers!
- Unlock at: 100 Light-Years
- Reset: Energy, Matter, Light-Years, all Generators
- Keep: Essence currency and upgrades

### Layer 2: Cosmic Prestige
Reset your Essence to gain **Celestial Shards**!
- Unlock at: 10 Essence minimum
- Reset: Essence and Essence multiplier
- Keep: Shards and permanent bonuses

## 💾 Save & Load

- **Auto-Save**: Every 10 seconds
- **Manual Save**: Click the Save button
- **Load Game**: Restore your progress
- **Hard Reset**: Start completely fresh

## 🎯 Strategy Tips

1. Click early to build momentum
2. Purchase generators for passive income
3. Use efficiency upgrades before buying generators
4. Prestige when you have enough currency for meaningful gains
5. Stack multipliers across prestige layers for exponential growth
6. Reach 1,000,000 Light-Years to complete the game!
